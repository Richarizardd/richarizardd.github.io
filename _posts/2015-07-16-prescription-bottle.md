Use them as travel containers for things!  
Normally people keep lids on their prescription containers like this.  
<figure>
	<img src="http://www.instructables.com/files/orig/F64/NNWE/F5LD8JPG/F64NNWEF5LD8JPG.jpg">
</figure>

But not many people know that on (some) containers, you can flip the cap upside down like this.
<figure>
	<img src="http://cdn.instructables.com/FYQ/HYL0/F5LD8JPH/FYQHYL0F5LD8JPH.LARGE.jpg">
</figure>

Which makes an airtight and liquidproof seal! Now you can use these prescription containers to store liquids such as shampoo, conditioner, lotion, etc. You can also use them to store quarters. I always carry a container of quarters with me wherever I go in case I need to buy something from a vending machine or pay at a coin only parking meter.
